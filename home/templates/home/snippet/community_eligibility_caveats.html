Smaller communities will operate differently than larger communities. Some of our rules may not apply to smaller communities.

Outreachy welcomes mentors from low-income countries and middle-income countries. We also welcome mentors from marginalized groups in the technology or science industry.

Outreachy acknowledges open source and open science is dominated by white, cis, western men. Communities built by marginalized groups in tech or people in low-income or middle-income countries may operate differently than communities built by privileged contributors. We encourage your community to apply, even if you are uncertain that your community will meet our eligibility rules.

If you have questions about if your community is eligible, please contact Outreachy organizers.
